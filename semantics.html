Semantic HTML and Page Meaning

One big thing I noticed from studying the template is how important semantic HTML is. Tags like <header>, <main>, <section>, and <footer> do more than just hold content. They help explain what each part of the page is meant to be.

In my own pages, I tried to follow this idea by wrapping the main content in <main>, placing the site title and menu inside <header>, and ending each page with a simple <footer>. This makes the code easier to understand when looking at it.

Semantic tags also help with accessibility and organization. Screen readers can move through the page better when the structure is clear. It also makes the site easier for me to maintain and update later.
